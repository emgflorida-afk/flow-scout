  • 1. Daily trend · setup direction matches Daily candle (UP for long, DOWN for short)
  • 2. Breakout/V-turn · multi-test breakout 60m confirmed OR named reversal pattern (hammer/shooter/V-turn)
  • 3. Tape aligned · live SPY/QQQ/IWM verdict matches direction (RISK_ON+long, RISK_OFF+short)
  • 4. 5-bar TA · 3+ of last 5 5m bars green for long, red for short
  • 5. Liquidity · resolved option contract has vol ≥ 100 OR OI ≥ 200
